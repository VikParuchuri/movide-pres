## Why Movide?

### The "old-style" LMS
* Not always mobile-friendly.
* Top-down style of teaching.
* Requires e-mail.
* Extremely powerful and rich.

### "Newer" style
* "Messy stream" of information.
* Data not under control of school.
* Not customizable.
* Very easy to use and student-friendly.
